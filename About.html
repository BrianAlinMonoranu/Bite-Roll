HISTORY OF BITE & ROLL

1990-Present

BITE & ROLL was opened by the bite brothers who where born in Kerry 1950. The shops started opening in 1990 and the first location was located in Parnell street in a small shop but after rented a different spot."We saw that burgers weren't served the way they should be so we opened a shop with this aspiration of providing quality service to the public at a reasonable price" - Charles Bite. After a few years Bite & Roll started franchising in Dublin and went over 1 Million in Sales in 2001. Currently around 3 Stores are opened in Dublin
